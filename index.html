Lights Out!

Clicks: 0

Instructions

Click on a square to toggle its color and that of its neighbors. The goal is to turn all the squares white. If you get stuck, click the reset button to start over. Good luck!

Addendum

The process of building this "Lights Out!" game involved various creative choices and challenges. Initially, a simple grid of squares was created, each with a click event listener to toggle its color and that of its neighbors. The initial challenge was to ensure the game started with a solvable configuration. This was solved by implementing a backtracking algorithm that simulates random clicks until a solution is found. To enhance user experience, a click counter and win condition with an alert were added. Further challenges included optimizing the backtracking algorithm for efficiency and ensuring the game board remains solvable after each click. These challenges were overcome by refining the algorithm and implementing checks for neighboring squares. Throughout the process, insights were gained into the importance of user experience, efficient algorithms, and robust error handling. Overall, the project provided a valuable learning experience in applying JavaScript to create a fun and engaging game.
